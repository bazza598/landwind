## Why this product won the screen

The vibration sleep alarm scored **5.00 / 5.00** on the decision model — top of a 10-product
WinningHunter scan (doc 01) — on: proven hot demand, ~71% gross margin (ad headroom),
single automatable SKU, no platform/legal friction, and open "heavy sleeper" brand whitespace.

**Honest risks:** "instant" still means paid ads + a ~£3–5k test budget; it's a white-label
commodity (moat = brand/CX/bundle, not the device); electronics carry defect/return load;
proven spend was EU/US so UK CPMs need a small validation test; COGS is an estimate until a
supplier confirms.

## SWOT (condensed)

- **Strengths:** proven hot demand (€52.8K validated creative, +453% ad growth); ~71% gross
  margin; single automatable SKU; mass-market pain → cheap broad targeting; clean compliant angle.
- **Weaknesses:** white-label commodity; electronics returns load; needs ad budget; zero trust/
  reviews yet; UK-only start; margin sensitive to CPM spikes.
- **Opportunities:** own the "heavy sleeper" story; kit AOV £55–65; subscription/replacements;
  B2B (hotels, hostels, shift-workers); TikTok/UGC organic; EU expansion once UK CPMs validate.
- **Threats:** copycat price war; incumbent (Histrips) outspends; Amazon private label; ad-account
  bans / attribution loss; rising CPMs; early negative reviews on defects.
