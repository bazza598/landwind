# Ad Angle Bank — WakeWave

**Core problem:** Heavy sleepers sleep through sound alarms; loud alarms wake partners and start
the day with a cortisol spike.
**Hero angle:** Empathy → Solution → Honest Proof ("You're not broken, your alarm is" → wrist
vibration that builds → 60-day guarantee, real reviews only).

Competitive evidence (WinningHunter, June 13): Histrips' empathy/heavy-sleeper angle carries
**€52.8K on one creative** (the same ad that read €40,306 on June 11 — it has grown). Angles 3,
6, 8 below are **unoccupied** — they run only 3 angle families.

## 5 full scripts

### Script 1 — Empathy (Angle 1) · 30s UGC video
**Hook (0–3s):** "If you've ever slept through THREE alarms… this isn't a discipline problem."
**Body:** "Your brain literally learns to filter out sound while you sleep. Mine did. What it
can't filter out is touch. [show wrist] This is WakeWave — it wakes you with vibration that
starts gentle and gets firmer until you're up. No sound. My girlfriend doesn't even stir."
**Proof:** "And if it doesn't wake you? 60 days, full refund, no quibble."
**CTA:** "Quiet mornings start at £34.99. Link below."

### Script 2 — Partner use case (Angle 3) · 25s UGC
**Hook:** "My husband's 5:30am alarm used to wake me. Every. Single. Day."
**Body:** "Then I bought him this — half as a gift, half in self-defence. It vibrates on HIS
wrist. He gets up for his shift, I hear nothing, and we stopped arguing about alarms entirely."
**CTA:** "WakeWave Couples 2-Pack — different wake times, zero noise."

### Script 3 — Problem agitation (Angle 7) · 35s voiceover
**Hook:** "6:00. Snooze. 6:09. Snooze. 6:18… you're already late and you haven't opened your eyes."
**Body:** "Every snooze fragments your sleep and every blaring restart jolts you awake stressed.
WakeWave replaces the whole mess: one silent vibration alarm on your wrist that builds until
you're up — no snooze spiral, no panic start."
**Proof/CTA:** "Designed for heavy sleepers. 60-day guarantee. From £34.99."

### Script 4 — Comparison (Angle 6) · 20s static/carousel
**Card 1:** "Phone alarm: wakes the whole house except you."
**Card 2:** "£300 smartwatch: needs charging nightly, pings you at 2am."
**Card 3:** "WakeWave: one job — wake you silently. £34.99. No app. No pairing."
**CTA:** "The simplest fix wins."

### Script 5 — Future pacing (Angle 8) · 30s cinematic
**Hook:** "Imagine tomorrow morning going like this…"
**Body:** "A gentle pulse on your wrist. You wake before it even reaches full strength. The room
is silent. Your partner's still asleep. You're up, on time, calm — and the day starts on your terms."
**Proof:** "That's the entire product. Nothing else. 60-day guarantee."
**CTA:** "WakeWave. Wake up. Quietly."

## Campaign structure

| Item | Spec |
|---|---|
| Structure | 1× Meta Advantage+ Sales campaign, UK only |
| Budget | £50/day test (£350–700 total test) |
| Creatives | 5 scripts above, 1 ad each, broad targeting |
| **Kill rule** (per ad, 72h / £30) | CTR < 1.5% or CPC > £1.20 → kill |
| **Hold rule** | CPA ≤ £20 single / ≤ £33 kit (i.e. under break-even) → keep |
| **Scale rule** | ROAS ≥ 2.0 over 3 days → +20% budget/day, duplicate winner with new hook |
| Compliance | Every ad clearly an ad; no invented stats; evidence filed for every claim |
